# Critic Rubric

**Score 0 (Missed)**: A fallacy was present but the critic failed to flag it.
**Score 1 (False Positive)**: The critic flagged a fallacy where none existed.
**Score 2 (Correct Detection)**: The critic correctly identified the fallacy type.
**Score 3 (Correct + Explanation)**: The critic correctly identified the fallacy AND provided a clear explanation of *why* it is problematic.

## Labels (L1-L6)

- **L1 Normative**: "Should", "Must" without authority.
- **L2 Framing**: Biased presentation of facts.
- **L3 Unsupported**: Claim without evidence.
- **L4 Contradiction**: Internal inconsistency.
- **L5 False Dilemma**: Presenting only two options when more exist.
- **L6 Slippery Slope**: Arguing A -> Z without proof.
